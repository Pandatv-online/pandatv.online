Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the minimum internet speed for watching channels?

Our speed recommendations:

    10 Mbit/s or more – for smooth SD channel viewing
    25 Mbit/s or more – for HD channels
    50 Mbit/s or more – for Full HD package
    150 Mbit/s or more – for 4K package (TorrServer)
2. How many devices can I use simultaneously?

You can use your subscription on up to two devices at the same time.

3. What is the channel archive and where can I find it?

The archive lets you rewatch your favorite shows and movies even if you missed the live broadcast. Panda TV currently offers a 4-day archive for all channels. Accessing the archive depends on the player or device you're using.

4. What playlist formats do you provide?

We provide three playlist formats based on your device/player:

    M3U8: The most common format, compatible with most players and devices.
    Samsung TV: Playlist for the default Samsung Smart TV player.
    Enigma Playlist: Format for devices running Enigma OS.
5. How do I add the playlist to my device?

If your player supports direct playlist URL input, we recommend using that method for automatic updates. If not, we’ll provide the playlist file for manual upload via USB or FTP.

6. What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, Revolut, Telegram Payments, cryptocurrency, and PayPal.

7. Which countries’ channels are available?

We offer channels from over 40 countries, including:

Australia, Armenia, Azerbaijan, Arab countries, Lithuania, Moldova, Poland, Portugal, Belarus, Brazil, UK, Germany, Romania, USA, Turkey, Uzbekistan, Georgia, Israel, India, Spain, Ukraine, Croatia, France, Sweden, Italy, Kazakhstan, Canada, Latvia, Russia, Finland, Estonia.

8. Can I use Panda TV while traveling or abroad?

Yes! You can watch from anywhere in the world as long as you have internet access. Just make sure your device and player are properly configured.

9. Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a free trial so you can test the service before subscribing. Contact us via Telegram, WhatsApp or e-mail to request one.

10. What devices are supported?

Panda TV works on Smart TVs, Android boxes, phones, tablets, computers, and even Enigma-based receivers. If your device supports m3u8 playlists — it works with us.

11. Is adult content included by default?

No. Adult content is optional and only activated upon request. We respect personal preferences and family-friendly viewing.

12. Can I get a custom playlist with only the channels I want?

Yes! Our Custom Plan allows you to select specific channels or groups. You choose exactly what you pay for — no unnecessary extras.

13. Is TorrServer legal and safe to use?

We don't promote or host copyrighted content. TorrServer is provided as an optional tool for users who want more flexibility.

14. Will my subscription renew automatically?

No. We don’t do auto-renewals. You’re in full control of when and how you pay. However, you can choose to pay for a full year in advance and get a 10% discount.

15. What if a channel stops working?

We monitor streams 24/7. If something goes wrong, report it via Telegram and we’ll fix it quickly or replace the stream.
